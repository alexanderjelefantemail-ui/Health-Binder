Walking Guide
Walk on flat even surfaces. Wear supportive cushioned shoes. Keep chin up, shoulders relaxed, and core gently engaged. Take shorter more frequent strides rather than long ones to reduce hip stress. Swing arms naturally.

If back or hip tightens: Stop, stand straight, do 5 slow hip circles each direction before continuing or heading home. Never push through sharp pain.
